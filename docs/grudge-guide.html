Tier System (T1–T8)

Every craftable item exists in 8 tiers. Higher tiers require higher-tier materials, profession levels, and crafting stations. Stats scale per tier using base + perTier × (tier − 1). The D5 rename moved "Legendary" from T5 to T8.

T1 Common
Bronze · Lv 1–5
T2 Uncommon
Silver · Lv 10–15
T3 Rare
Blue · Lv 20–25
T4 Epic
Purple · Lv 35–40
T5 Heroic
Red · Lv 50–55
T6 Mythic
Orange · Lv 65–70
T7 Ancient
Gold · Lv 80–85
T8 Legendary
Shimmer · Lv 95
Crafting Stations: T1–T5 use standard stations (Anvil, Sawmill, Loom, etc.). T6–T7 require Master stations. T8 requires Divine stations and Divine Essence. T7–T8 recipes are drop-only (recipe scrolls from bosses).

4 Classes — Weapon Restrictions

Warrior

Melee specialist. Stamina system tied to sprint → invisible target lock, speed boost, charge attacks. Stamina fills through parries, dodges, blocks. Perfect actions grant extra. Can double jump, AoE attacks, group invincibility.
No weapon restrictions — can equip any weapon type

Mage

Ranged caster. Can create special particle teleport blocks (max 10), placeable by AI companions, breakable by other factions, limited health. Specializes in elemental and arcane magic.
StaffTomeMace WandOff-hand Relic

Ranger

Ranged & melee hybrid. RMB + LMB triggers attempted parry → perfect parry = counter. Players get instant dash attack while enemy stunned 0.5s (2s window). NPCs have different stun duration.
BowCrossbowGun DaggerGreatswordSpear

Worge

Shapeshifter. Three forms: Bear (large, powerful), Raptor (invisible, rogue-like), Large Bird (flyable, mountable by players/AI). Nature/primal magic hybrid.
StaffSpearDagger BowHammerMace Off-hand RelicScythe

Recipe Categories (WCS Source of Truth)

Every craftable (non-gathered) item needs a recipe. The WCS CSV has 109 explicit recipes. Full coverage requires 600+ when all weapon types × tiers, armor × tiers, and enchants are included.

T0 starter recipes: T0 recipes are universal starter crafts. Everyone can make them at the shared starter crafting site that supports the T0→T1 onboarding flow, including the current auto-harvest starter loop and baseline starter outputs for armor, capes, potions, foods, and related basics.
