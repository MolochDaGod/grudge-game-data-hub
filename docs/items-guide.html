Tier System (T1 - T8)

Tiers scale craftable items. Every base weapon expands into all 8 tiers with scaling stats. "Legendary" now means T8 only - the old T5 label was renamed to Heroic.

T1 Common

Starter gear. Craftable with basic materials.

T2 Uncommon

Slight stat upgrade. Early progression.

T3 Rare

Notable stat jump. Mid-game.

T4 Epic

Strong items with special properties.

T5 Heroic

End-game crafts and rare drops.was "Legendary"

T6 Mythic

Faction-specific high-end gear.

T7 Ancient

Ancient relics with unique abilities.

T8 Legendary

Apex of the crafting track.was "Legendary Artifact"

Naming Rules (reserved words)

Certain words now have exactly one meaning in the data. Do not reuse them as lore names, flavor descriptors, or category slugs.

WordMeansRule
LegendaryT8 tier label on tiered items.Never used as a category or weapon-type name.
HeroicT5 tier label on tiered items.Replaces the old "T5 Legendary" label; the word "Legendary" is reserved for T8.
ArtifactWeapon category for end-game, world-found items.Not a tier label. Never appears in the tier string.
ArcaneA spell school AND an artifact sub-type (artifact-arcane).No longer names a weapon class. The old arcaneStaves slot is now artifact.

Off-hand utility

Tomes offhandno tier

Tomes are tier-less off-hand items. Equipping a tome while wielding a 1h main-hand (sword, axe, dagger, mace, pistol) grants that main-hand new spell-cast options drawn from the tome's school.

type: "offhand-tome" · skillGrants: [...] · No stat block.

Artifacts (end-game, world-found)

Artifact worldhidden

Artifacts are end-game items with fixed stats and no craft recipe. They are discovered in the world: boss drops, raid vaults, world reveals, or quest completions. Player-facing UIs filter out artifacts with discovery.hiddenUntilFound: true until the player has found them.

First sub-type: artifact-arcane (the old arcaneStaves slot). Future sub-types reserved: storm, blood, frost, ember, nature, holy, void.

Consumables & Materials

Food Chef

Red / Green / Blue food colors. Buffs scale with level.

Potions Mystic

Restores, resistances, and short-duration buffs.

Materials gather

Ore, ingots, logs, cloth, leather, essence, gem, herb. Each tier has its own material tree.

Artifact Discovery Rules

Rule Undiscovered artifacts must not leak into player-facing UIs. The discovery block on each artifact includes hiddenUntilFound, source, and revealCondition. Client code checks a player's discovered-artifact set before rendering an artifact anywhere a player can see. Admin tools bypass this filter.
