Gathering Professions (6)

Resource collection from the world. Levels 1-100 with 5 progression tiers. Each profession unlocks T1-T8 resources as you level.

Extract ores, gems, and minerals. Feeds into Miner, Engineer, Mystic professions.

Crafting Professions (5)

Specializations that consume gathered materials to create equipment, consumables, and structures.

⛏️ Miner

Forge weapons and heavy armor from ores. Creates: Swords, Axes, Daggers, Hammers, Greatswords, Greataxes, Plate Armor. Smelts raw ore into refined ingots.
Synergies: Mining, Scavenging

🌲 Forester

Craft bows, leather gear, and ship parts. Creates: Bows, Leather Armor, Hunting Daggers, Ship Components. Mills planks and tans leather.
Synergies: Logging, Skinning, Herbalism

🔮 Mystic

Enchant items and weave magic cloth. Creates: Staves, Cloth Armor, Enchantments, Scrolls, Grimoires, Soul Gems. Weaves magic fabric from fibers.
Synergies: Mining (Gems), Herbalism, Fishing

👨‍🍳 Chef

Cook food buffs and brew potions. Creates: Buff Food (red/blue/green), Potions, Elixirs, Preserved Rations, Antidotes.
Synergies: Fishing, Skinning, Herbalism

🔧 Engineer

Build ranged weapons and siege equipment. Creates: Crossbows, Firearms, Grenades, Traps, Scopes, Siege Equipment, Automata.
Synergies: Mining, Scavenging, Logging
